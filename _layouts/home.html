About Me

Down to Business

I help businesses design and build the right products for their customers. I'm a chamption for design and data driven products, a consensus builder, and a UX advocate.

I see every day as an opportunity to do the best work of my life.

User/Customer Experience Design

I bring a customer-centric approach to every project I tackle. Using a combination of research, design thinking, and cross-team collaboration, I provide business value by identifying and solving the real problem behind the symptom.

Design/Research OPS

Bureaucracy I can do without. Processes, guidelines, and systems of work are essential to project success.

  • The right guidelines to ensure productivity while allowing essential creativity
  • The right ceremonies to establish and grow teams
  • The right methods for sharing the research generated insights

My motto for Design/Research OPS is much like the Agile process itself, ship light and fast, then iterate.

Team Leadership and Management

I make companies and projects better through decisive, responsive leadership, through constructive process and with a passion for people by:

  • Running and developing UX Design and Research teams.
  • Overseeing, facilitating, and directing company-wide efforts, building consensus and fostering a shared vision for CX/UX.
  • Investing in, learning from, and mentoring my teammates and direct reports.
  • Knowing my team members and how to use their skills for their personal satisfaciton and the good of the business.

How my teams and I have won

Design Systems

As Director of User Experience and User Interface, I oversaw and guided the development and launch of our company's first Design System: a robust, fully realized design system built in React. It features everything a growing company needs for world-class application development:

  • Fully developed documentation site with robust designer, tech-writer, and developer resources.
  • Lockstep designer resource library ensures that developer implementation matches mockups 100%.
  • Custom Iconic font for great market customization and iconography.
  • Accessibility features for both the hearing and visually impaired.
  • Consistent UX/UI across the entire library.
  • Built-in support for i18N transaltions.
  • Atom component design.
  • Light & dark themes.
